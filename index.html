Heat index calculator

Today it's hot, we are in May and already 31°C, that's too much for me. I talked with a colleague from Marocco, he told me that for him 31 is also too hot, but in Marocco when it's 40 or 50°C it's perfectly bearable, I wondered why.. turned out it's mostly due to the relative humidity in the air. To cool down our body will sweat and evacuate this heat. To evaporate this sweat must have room in the air, ie the air must be dry enough to allow perspiration and therefore the cooling of the metabolism. The apparent temperature is therefore higher when the humidity is higher. I build a small webapp to calculate this apparent temperature also known as heat index. .
